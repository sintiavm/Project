WE BOOST YOUR COMPANY

The Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Arequipa was founded in the city of Arequipa on September 23, 1887, according to the minutes signed at the home of Messrs. Zizold Colsman y Cía. at 7pm. the same day. We are the union organization that represents the business activity of the Arequipa region. We support our associates in creating value in their economic activities, increasing the competitiveness of their companies, representing and defending their interests, thus promoting their economic development. We provide services and products with a high level of efficiency, using modern and innovative technologies
